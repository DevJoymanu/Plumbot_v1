Privacy Policy

Last updated 21 July 2026
HomeX Media is currently operating under this trading name while formal business registration is completed. This policy will be updated with our registered company details once that process finishes; it is accurate and in effect in the meantime.

Who we are

Plumbot is a WhatsApp-based assistant platform operated by HomeX Media ("we", "us"). We provide the platform to independent service businesses ("client businesses") so they can use WhatsApp to talk to their own customers — answering questions, quoting prices, and booking appointments.

When you message a client business's WhatsApp number, HomeX Media processes that conversation on their behalf, as their service provider. The client business is responsible for its own customers and decides what it uses our platform for; we handle the data as instructed by them.

What we collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and profile name.
  • The content of messages you send to a client business's number — text, photos, videos, voice notes, and documents.
  • Information you provide in conversation to get a quote or book an appointment: name, service address/area, job details, preferred date/time, and similar booking information.
  • Basic technical metadata needed to deliver messages (message IDs, timestamps, delivery status).

How we use it

  • To understand your message and generate a relevant reply (we use an AI language model provider to help interpret and draft responses).
  • To schedule and remind you about appointments you book.
  • To store any photos or documents you send (e.g. a site plan) so the client business can refer back to them.
  • To send related emails (e.g. booking confirmations) where you've provided an email address.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your conversations to train third-party AI models beyond generating your own reply.

Who we share it with

We use the following providers to operate the service. Each only receives what's needed to perform its function:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform — transports the messages themselves.
  • DeepSeek (AI provider) — processes message text to classify intent and help generate replies.
  • Cloudflare — stores photos, documents, and other media you send.
  • Our email provider — sends transactional emails (e.g. booking confirmations) where an email address is provided.
  • Google Calendar — only if a client business has enabled calendar sync for its own appointments.
  • Railway — hosts the application and database.

How long we keep it

We retain conversation and booking data for as long as needed to provide the service to the client business you contacted, and as reasonably required for their own record-keeping (e.g. completed job history). If you'd like your data removed, contact us using the details below and we'll action it, subject to any records the client business is legally required to keep.

Your choices

You can ask the client business — or us directly — what data we hold about you, request a copy, or ask for it to be deleted. You can also simply stop messaging the number at any time.
